Postpartum Doula · Waco, TX

You Don't Have to Do This Alone

A postpartum doula is the steady, experienced presence every new parent deserves — and very few know they can have.

What Is a Postpartum Doula?

A postpartum doula is a trained professional who supports families during the weeks and months after birth. Unlike a night nurse or nanny, a doula cares for the whole family — not just the baby.

I help with the emotional adjustment, answer the questions you didn't know you'd have, and provide hands-on support so you can rest, recover, and bond with your baby.

💛

Emotional Support

A listening ear, a calm presence, and reassurance that what you're feeling is completely normal.

👶

Newborn Care

Diapering, soothing, bathing, safe sleep — I'll help you feel confident with your baby.

🤱

Feeding Support

Whether breastfeeding, pumping, or bottle-feeding, I'll help you find what works for your family.

What to Expect

Every family is different, so we start with a prenatal visit to understand your needs, your values, and your vision for the postpartum period. After your baby arrives, I visit your home weekly to provide hands-on help and emotional support.

My goal is simple: by the time our work together ends, you feel confident, rested, and connected to your baby.

Service Area

In-home visits are available in Waco, TX and surrounding areas (approximately 50-mile radius). Virtual postpartum support is available nationwide.

Pediatric Sleep Consulting

Better Sleep Starts Here

You're exhausted. Your baby is exhausted. It doesn't have to stay this way. I'll help your family sleep better — gently, respectfully, and at your own pace.

My Approach

I believe every child can learn to sleep well — without crying it out, without rigid schedules, and without ignoring your instincts as a parent.

My gentle approach is built around your child's temperament, your family's values, and the science of infant sleep. There's no one-size-fits-all plan. Every sleep plan I create is custom.

How It Works

1. Intake questionnaire — Before we meet, you'll fill out a detailed form about your child's sleep habits, schedule, and your family's goals.

2. Consultation call — We meet via video to discuss everything in depth. I'll ask questions, listen, and start building your plan.

3. Custom sleep plan — Within 48 hours, you'll receive a detailed, step-by-step plan tailored to your child and your comfort level.

4. Ongoing support — For package clients, I'm with you daily for two weeks via text and email, plus follow-up calls to adjust the plan as needed.

Available Worldwide

All sleep consulting sessions are conducted via video call, so I can work with families anywhere in the United States and internationally.

About Me

Hi, I'm Silvia.

I know what it feels like to wake up at 3 a.m. wondering if you're doing everything wrong. I know the loneliness of those early weeks, when everyone says "enjoy every moment" but no one asks how you're really doing.

That experience changed me. It's why I became a postpartum doula and sleep consultant — to be the calm, knowledgeable, judgment-free presence I wished I'd had.

I believe that when a mother is supported, the whole family thrives. And I believe every baby can learn to sleep well, gently, when we understand their unique needs.
